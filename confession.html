Prayer and Fasting 2022

This site is the publication of the Prayer Cards used during the week of Prayer and Fasting. Please use them as a guide during your time with the Lord as we keep the Intercession going for 2022!

Scripture for 2022

"I sought the Lord and He answered me and delivered me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." (Psalm 34:4-5, ESV)

  • Confession of Confidence in Jesus

    • Father, in the name of Jesus, I hold to Your Word as final authority in my life. I will not consider the appearance of things - the adverse circumstances of the world - or evil reports. I will follow the things of the spirit and not the things of the flesh. My confidence is in Jeus!

    • My heart is fixed, established, confident in your provision and I refuse to be moved by anything that tries to exalt itself against your Word.

    • Jesus is Lord over my life - I refuse to waver. He is Lord over spirit, body, and mind. He is Lord of my finances. I recognize His Lordship on a daily basis.

    • I can boldly and confidently say, "I have no lack, I walk in abundance. I am in safety and quiet from fear and evil. I am healed. I have wisdom. The joy of the Lord is my strength. Everything I put my hands to is a success in Jesus' Name."

    • I am committed to consider Jesus in every decision in my life - great or small. I will follow after the Word of God and I refuse to compromise.

    • I give Jesus first place in my life.
