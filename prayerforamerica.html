Prayer and Fasting 2022

This site is the publication of the Prayer Cards used during the week of Prayer and Fasting. Please use them as a guide during your time with the Lord as we keep the Intercession going for 2022!

Scripture for 2022

"I sought the Lord and He answered me and delivered me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." (Psalm 34:4-5, ESV)

Prayer for America

  • Heavenly Father, I come to You now in the Name of Jesus. I thank You that I can abide under the shadow of my Almighty God. I thank You that a thousand may fall at my side and ten thousand at my right hand, but none will come near me. I thank You that You have given Your angels charge over me to keep me in all of my ways.

  • Heavenly Father, I pray for the President of this nation, all of the Executive Branch of this nation, and all members of Congress of this nation, all of the leadership of all local, state, regional, and federal agencies, commissions, and governments of this nation. I ask You to guide and direct the leadership of this nation in every way.

  • Lord, I thank You for Your protective hand that has shielded our nation. Thank You, Lord, for hearing the prayers and requests of Your people on behalf of this nation. I pray in the Name of Jesus, asking You to give ys divine protection for Your people of this nation, according to John 16:23. I'm asking You to ensure the safety of Your people and to keep us, and our nation, from harm's way and provide protection from plans of destruction that have been plotted against us. I pray, Father, that You would extend a hand of protection against all of the destructive forces of terrorism against Your people and this nation. I pray that You will protect us from evil attacks or acts of terrorism and stop the terrorists that attempt to bring destruction against us.

  • We proclaim that our help comes from the Lord. In Him we trust. Amen!

Prayer Against Terrorism

  • Heavenly Father, may the knowledge of terrorist's planned attacks be revealed to those who provided our national and international security. I pray that You would give wisdom and discernment to those who provide protection for Your people and this nation. Help everyone in authority to be alert to signs of wrongdoings in this nation. I pray that You will provide insight to national, international, and local authorities of this nation, to at swiftly, to avert all danger, protecting American lives and property. I pray that You will provide instruction in the development of effectual and efficient anti-terrorist strategies and provide our nation with an arsenal of weapons that will give our nation an advantage against any aggressor coming against this nation or Your people. Guide the authorities of this nation in efforts to seek out and eradicate terrorists. Enable the military forces of this nation to become swift, powerful, and accurate in any action of intelligence gathering and retaliation. I submit this to Your hand, Lord. I give You the praise and I give You the glory that You hear my prayers and my supplications. Heavenly Father, I pray that every potential acts of terrorism against this nation or Your People will be exposed. Heavenly Father, I ask You to loose Your mighty warring and guardian angels to expose and destroy any potential acts of terrorism against any city, any town, or any of Your people of this nation. I ask You to bless and strengthen this nation. I ask You to cause confusion in the minds of all terrorist who are plotting against this nation, or any of Your people in this nation. I ask You to do all of these things according to John 14:14.

  • Heavenly FAther, You are our refuge and stronghold in times of trouble. So, I declare with my mouth that Your people of this nation will dwll safely in this land and prosper abundantly. I am more than a conqueror through Christ Jesus. I declare that no weapon formed against me shall prosper. (Isaiah 54:17) Amen!

Prayer for Our President and Our Government

  • Father, in Jesus' name, we give thanks for our country and its government. We hold up in prayer before You the men and women who are in positions of authority. We pray and intercede for the President, the representatives, the senators, the jedges of our land, the policeman, as well as the governors and mayors, and for all those who are in authority over us in any way. We pray that the Spirit of the Lord rests upon them.

  • Father, we ask that You surround the President with men and women who make their hearts and ears attentive to Godly counsel and do that which is right in Your sight. We believe You cause them to be men and women of integrity who are obedient concerning us that we may lead a quiet adn peaceable life in all godliness adn honesty. We pray that the upright shall dwell in our government ... that men and women blameless and complete in You sight, Father, shall remain in these positions of authority; but the wicked shall be cut off of our government and the treacherous shall be rooted out of it.

  • Your Word declares that "Blessed is the nation whose God is the Lord... ". We receive Your blessing. Father, You are our refuge and stronghold in times of trouble (high cost, destitution, and desperation). So, we declare with our mouths that Your people shall dwell safely in this land and we prosper abundantly. We are more than conquerors through Christ Jesus!

  • Jesus is Lord over the United States!
