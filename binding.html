Prayer and Fasting 2022

This site is the publication of the Prayer Cards used during the week of Prayer and Fasting. Please use them as a guide during your time with the Lord as we keep the Intercession going for 2022!

Scripture for 2022

"I sought the Lord and He answered me and delivered me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." (Psalm 34:4-5, ESV)

Prayer for Binding and Loosing

  • I have the keys of the kingdom, and whatever I bind on earth is bound in heaven, and whatever I loose on earth is loosed in heaven (Matthew 16:19).

  • I bind proud spirits arrayed against my life (Job 41:5).

  • I bind all sickness and disease released against my mind and body.

  • I loose my mind, will, and emotions from every assignment and spirit of darkness in the name of Jesus.

  • I loose my finances from every spirit of poverty, debt, and lack.

  • I command all hidden spirits hiding in amy part of my life to come out in the name of Jesus.

  • I loose myself from all generational curses and hereditary spirits. (Galatians 3:13)

  • I loose myself from every negative word spoken against my life.

Prayer for Deliverance from Strongholds

  • Heavenly Father,

  • I come to you in the MIGHTY of JESUS. I desire to destroy every ungodly stronghold in my life. I know the foundation must be completely destroyed to avoid another strondgold from being erected.

  • According to Matthew 12:29. I bind the strongman of _________________________.

  • I dismantle, uproot, and take dominion over every thought process and faulty mindset contrary to the Word of God.

  • I apply the precious blood of Jesus over my life and I forbid satan from operating through me. I know it is your will for me to be set free from every ungodly stronghold. Therefore, I request and trust the HOLY SPIRIT to reveal to me every stronghold and the source of its foundation

  • I loose deliverance and freedom adn liberty in my life in Jesus' name.

Loose Thyself from Unforgiveness and Bitterness

  • Unforgiveness opens the door for tormenting spirits (Matthew 18). Bitterness opens the door for spirits of infirmity, including arthritis adn cancer. Unforgiveness is the result of being hurt, rejected, abandoned, disappointed, abused, raped, molested, taken advantage of, lied on, cheated, talked about, etc.

  • LOOSE THYSELF - SELF-DELIVERANCE

  • I command all spirits operating in my blood and circulatory systems to come out in the name of Jesus.
  • I command all spirits operating in my muscles and muscular system to come out in the name of Jesus.
  • I command all religious spirits of doubt, unbelief, error, heresy, and tradition that came in through religion to come out in the name of Jesus.
  • I command all spirits from my past that are hindering my present and future to come out in the name of Jesus.
  • I command all ancestral spirits that entered through my ancestors to come out in the name of Jesus.
