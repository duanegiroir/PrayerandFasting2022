Pray and Fasting 2022

This site is the publicaiton of the Prayer Cards used during the week of Prayer and Fasting. Please use them as a guide during your time with the Lord as we keep the Intercession going for 2022!

Scripture for 2022

"I sought the Lord and He answered me and delived me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." (Psalm 34:4-5, ESV)

  • Declare the Benefits of Fasting Over Your Life

    • Lord, I believe teh power of Your chosen fast. (Isaiah 58)
    • Lord, let my fasting destroy the yokes that the enemy has set up againt me.
    • Ley Your light come into my life through Your choses fast.
    • Let Health and healing be released to me through your chosen fast.
    • Let miracles be released in my life through Your Chosen Fast.
    • I humble my soul through fasting: ley Your favor exalt me.
    • Let Your covenant blessing and mercy be released on me through this fast.
    • Nothing is impossible with You, Lord. Let my impossibilities become possiblities through Your chosen fast.
    • Let Your anointing increase in my life through Your chosen fast.
    • Let my prayers be answered speedily through Your chosen fast.
    • Manifest Your glory to me through Your chosen fast.
    • Let all poverty and lack be destroyed in my life through fasting.

  • Facts Worth Remembering about Fasting

    • Fasting is a New Covenant command and choice for every believer.
    • Spiritual growth can be greatly accelerated by fasting.
    • Fasting is like a nuclear bomb to the mountain that refuses to move by any other means.
    • Fasting without prayer is just dieting.
    • Frustration is the fruit of fasting without prayer.
    • Experience is the best teacher in prayer and fasting.
    • Fasting is the Darkroom of Discovery.
    • Dreams are often developed in the dark.
    • Fasting is to the believer as fire is to steel.
    • Knowing the Word of God builds faith for answered prayer.
