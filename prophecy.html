Prayer and Fasting 2022

This site is the publication of the Prayer Cards used during the week of Prayer and Fasting. Please use them as a guide during your time with the Lord as we keep the Intercession going for 2022!

Scripture for 2022

"I sought the Lord and He answered me and delivered me from all my fears. Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." (Psalm 34:4-5, ESV)

  • Psalms 91

    • He that dwellth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty.

    • I will say of the Lord, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust

    • Surely he shall deliver thee from the snare of the fowler, and from the noisome pestilence.

    • He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust: his truth shall be thy shield and buckler.

    • Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night; nor for the arrow that flieth by day;

    • Nor for the pestilence that walketh in darkness; nor for the destruction that wasteth at noonday.

    • A thousand shall fall at my side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee.

    • Only with thine eyes shalt thour behold and see the reward of the wicked.

    • Because thou hast made the Lord, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy habitation;

    • There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling.

    • For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways.

    • They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a stone.

    • Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: the young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet.

    • Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.

    • He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him and honour him.

    • With long life will I satisfy him, and show him my salvation.

Prophcy Recieved by Pastor Wanda Rousse During the Week of Prayer and Fasting for Faith Cathedral - January 16, 2019 (1:33 PM)

  • My people, My people, My people -

  • I call you mine because you are Mine indeed. You are beginning to see how powerful and how needful your prayers are.

  • The world is filled with chaos and turmoil. Oh, but I say I have not changed. Nor have I changed my plans. I have had a plan including your role in it since the foundation of the world.

  • I have filled your heart and your mind with My word, which is exceedingly Powerful

  • I placed you and your seed on this Earth for such a time as this.

  • I now see many of you as Prayer Warriors. You are fully equipped to Bring Change. You will boldly proclaim My word. Great testimonies shall come forth in 2019. Souls will surrender as a result of your testimony.

  • Because of sincere hearts crying out to Me and sacrifice that has not gone unnoticed. My heat is delighted to release the answers to cause the chains to break.

  • Tonight I am causing ungodly releationships to fall permanently.

  • Sons and daughters will return to their natural state and begin to worship Me.

  • Your prayers have formed a border. The enemy can no longer seduce and rob adn steal from your seed because a border has been established through your prayer.

  • They will return as the Prodigal Son. When they return, love them - for they are Mine!! - saith the Lord.
